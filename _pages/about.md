I am an engineering student at the University of Cambridge with a developing interest in research, particularly in control theory, multi-agent systems, and related mathematical frameworks.

My work and studies focus on systems theory, networked control, and the mathematical structures that arise in distributed and interacting systems. I am particularly interested in connections between control theory, topology, and modern approaches to complex engineered systems.

This website serves as a place to document projects, research ideas, technical notes, and academic activities as they develop.

- research projects and collaborations  
- technical notes and essays  
- publications or preprints
